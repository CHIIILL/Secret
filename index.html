About Us

Meet with Intellectero, a FinTech, DeFi, NFT, and Blockchain Development Company

Intellectero is a full-stack development company that operates in FinTech, DeFi, NFT, and Blockchain industries. Our main benefit is an ability to develop projects from a scratch on a turnkey basis: we can implement your idea into real life and provide you will a completely finished product that is ready for use. Intellectero was initially established in Great Britain. 2 years later, the company moved to Turkey. Here we gathered the best specialists, and now our team consists of more than 50 members, among which are skilled software developers, project managers, designers, and others. Each member of the Intellectero team has 10+ years of experience, a huge portfolio, and strong hard skills. Our professionals have successfully worked with Smart Donation Coin, Zeus Group, QSoft, Otto Group, BitOnyx Labs Limited, and other well-known projects.
